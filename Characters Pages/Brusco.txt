[1] He has two daughters, Brea and Talea , and at least two sons. He is sparing with his words and his coin. [2]   Contents   1  Recent Events   1.1  A Feast for Crows  1.2  A Dance With Dragons    2  References    Recent Events  A Feast for Crows  Arya Stark is sent to work for Brusco the fishmonger who sells his stock below the wharves of the Drowned Town. She is sent to him to learn the Braavosi tongue. [1] Brusco arrives at the fishmarket with Arya in tow, going through the daily catch and choosing which to buy. While Brusco sleeps, his daughter Brea visits with a boy on the rooftop. [2]   A Dance With Dragons  Arya Stark returns to work for Brusco, after having been training at the House of Black and White , as if she had never been away in order to carry out an assassination.
