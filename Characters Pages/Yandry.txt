His wife is Ysilla . He is an orphan of the Greenblood who has returned to Mother Rhoyne . He has a Rhoynish cast to his features. [1]   Recent Events  A Dance with Dragons  Yandry and his wife transport Griff and his party down the Rhoyne to Volantis .
