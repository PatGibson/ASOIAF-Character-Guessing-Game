 Aemon Targaryen 
 
 From A Wiki of Ice and Fire 
 
 
Jump to:  navigation ,  search 
 
 Aemon Targaryen  can refer to multiple members of  House Targaryen :
 
 Prince  Aemon Targaryen , eldest son of King Jaehaerys I Targaryen and Queen Alysanne Targaryen, father to Princess Rhaenys Targaryen, who also known as  the Queen Who Never Was 
 Prince  Aemon Targaryen , called  Aemon the Dragonknight , a son of Viserys II and a member of the Kingsguard for four kings 
 Prince  Aemon Targaryen , son of King Maekar I Targaryen, later in life known simply as Maester Aemon, sworn to the Night's Watch
