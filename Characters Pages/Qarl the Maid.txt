 Qarl , widely known as  Qarl the Maid , is an  ironborn  warrior sworn to  Balon Greyjoy . [1]  He is the grandson of a  thrall . [2]

 Appearance and Character 
 See also:  Images of Qarl 
 Qarl the Maid is beardless and pink-cheeked. [1] [3]  His straight sandy hair brushes against his shoulders. [2]  He has a lean swimmer's physique. [2] 
 Qarl is renowned as one of the fiercest warriors of the  Iron Islands . [4]  He wields a longsword but is not an experienced horse rider. [2] 
 
 History 
 When  Asha Greyjoy  first met Qarl in  293 AC  or  294 AC , she called his attempt at growing a beard "peach fuzz". Since he had never seen a peach, she encouraged him to join her crew. They traded at  Fair Isle ,  Lannisport , and the  Arbor , [2]  and raided  Lyseni  pirates in the  Stepstones . [5]  Qarl and Asha began sleeping together after sampling peaches at the Arbor. [2] 
 
 Recent Events 
 A Clash of Kings 
 Qarl and two companions,  Skyte  and  Uller , encounter  Theon  and  Asha Greyjoy  in  Lordsport . There they recount the story of the death of Skyte's brother,  Eldiss . Asha tells Theon that he should make them part of his crew. [6] 
 
 A Feast for Crows 
 Qarl serves aboard Asha's ship, the  Black Wind . [7]  During the  kingsmoot , he wins the  finger dance  against  Eldred Codd . [5]  Qarl is one of the champions for Asha at the kingsmoot, but  Euron Greyjoy  is instead chosen  King of the Isles and the North . [3] 
 
 A Dance with Dragons 
 Qarl sails to  Deepwood Motte  with Asha as she contemplates her next move. Qarl sleeps with Asha in  Galbart Glover 's bedchamber. [2]  He is captured along with Asha,  Tris Botley , and several other ironborn by the army of  Stannis Baratheon  in the  fight by Deepwood Motte . [8] 
 Qarl is later freed and ransomed along with the other ironborn by  Tycho Nestoris , as the  Braavosi banker  needs an escort to get him to Stannis whose army is  marching on Winterfell . [9]
