In the television adaptation Game of Thrones she is played by Amrita Acharia . [2]   Contents   1  Appearance  2  History  3  Recent Events   3.1  A Game of Thrones  3.2  A Clash of Kings  3.3  A Storm of Swords  3.4  A Dance with Dragons    4  Quotes  5  References    Appearance  See also: Images of Irri  Irri has skin the color of copper, with black hair and "almond-shaped eyes". [3] She, along with Jhiqui , are of an age with Daenerys Targaryen . [4]   History  Irri was originally a member of a rival khalasar which was defeated by Khal Drogo . She was enslaved . [4]   Recent Events  A Game of Thrones   Amrita Acharia as Irri, in the HBO adaptation  Irri, Doreah , and Jhiqui are given to Daenerys Targaryen by Viserys Targaryen as wedding gifts to serve as her handmaids. She is given to Daenerys to teach her how to ride. [3] She stays with Daenerys, even after the death of Khal Drogo and the disintegration of his khalasar . [5]   A Clash of Kings  After struggling through the red waste , Irri and Jhiqui attempt to convince Daenerys not to stay in Vaes Tolorro , believing it haunted by ghosts. [6]   A Storm of Swords  Irri becomes a paramour to the lonely Daenerys. Daenerys feels guilt for this. Irri is present when Daenerys purchases the Unsullied . Irri and Jhiqui free Rhaegal and Viserion to attack the Astapori slavers when Dany has the Unsullied attack their former masters.  A Dance with Dragons  Irri and Jhiqui both develop a crush on Rakharo who has grown more tall and muscular. Both argue whom he would prefer. [7]  Irri accompanies Daenerys to Daznak's Pit as part of the royal procession. [8] She and Jhiqui both depart Meereen as part of the search parties dispatched across the Skahazadhan to locate Daenerys when she disappears on Drogon 's back. [9]   Quotes  It is known.
