Contents   1  Appearance  2  Recent Events   2.1  A Game of Thrones  2.2  A Storm of Swords  2.3  A Dance with Dragons    3  References    Appearance  Othell is lantern-jawed. [1]   Recent Events  A Game of Thrones  Othell is present as First Builder when the new members of the Night's Watch swear their vows. [1]   A Storm of Swords  The brothers of the Night's Watch left to defend the Wall from the assault by the wildlings name one of the straw dummies after him. [2] During the choosing of the new Lord Commander of the Night's Watch , Othell puts his name forth. [3] In the end he withdraws his name from the election, though he also refuses to support Janos Slynt 's candidacy. [4]   A Dance with Dragons  Othell and his builders are sent by Lord Commander Jon Snow to restore the Nightfort .
